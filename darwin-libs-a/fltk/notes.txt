This is fltk 1.3.3, downloaded from:
http://fltk.org/pub/fltk/1.3.3/fltk-1.3.3-source.tar.gz

I set the following settings in CMake:
CMAKE_OSX_ARCHITECTURES=i386;x86_64
CMAKE_OSX_DEPLOYMENT_TARGET=10.6
CMAKE_OSX_SYSROOT=/Applications/Xcode.app/Contents/Developer/Platforms/MacOSX.platform/Developer/SDKs/MacOSX10.6.sdk
OPTION_BUILD_EXAMPLES=OFF
OPTION_USE_GL=OFF
OPTION_USE_SYSTEM_LIBJPEG=OFF
OPTION_USE_SYSTEM_LIBPNG=OFF
OPTION_USE_SYSTEM_ZLIB=ON
